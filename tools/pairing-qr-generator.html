SMS Sync — Pairing QR

Paste your Supabase project URL and Publishable key, generate a QR, then scan it from the app's host setup (or client pairing) screen. Runs entirely local — nothing is uploaded. 


  

    The QR encodes JSON of the form {"url":"...","key":"..."}, matching the
    app's pairing format. Anyone who scans it gets full access to that Supabase project,
    so share it carefully.
